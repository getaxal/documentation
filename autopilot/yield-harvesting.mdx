Automatically capture and reinvest yields from your crypto assets to maximize returns.

## Features
* **Automatic Collection**: Harvests profits at regular intervals (e.g., every X days)
* **Flexible Deployment**:  Choose between:
    * Direct transfer to your specified wallet
    * Reinvestment into stable yield platforms (e.g. Aave, Compound, etc.)

## Benefits
* Compounds returns automatically
* Reduces manual management overhead
* Optimizes yield-generating opportunities for different assets

## How it works
1. System monitors your portfolio for accumulated yields
2. At predetermined intervals, yields are automatically collected
3. Based on your settings, profits are either:
    * Transferred to your designated wallet
    * Reinvested into selected yield-generating platforms
